Welcome. This is a trainer for the NZART Exam which is required to gain a New Zealand Amateur Radio Licence.
The questions in this exam are from version NZARTX17f which was issued on April 2018.

Please be aware that this trainer is not affiliated with New Zealand Association of Radio Transmitters.
Once you're able to complete this exam reliably, please see their website to sit the real exam.
